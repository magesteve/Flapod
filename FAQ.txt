What is Flapod?
Flapod stands for Flutter Another Picture of the Day app, an experiment in the use  of Flutter, an open-source UI software development kit created by Google. Fapod was created to be an example of how to create a helpful app on multiple platforms (iOS, MacOS, Android, VisionOS).
Who created Fapod?
Steve Sheets, a long-time developer of desktop and mobile applications, created Fapod. As much as Fapod was an experiment in Flutter, it was also an experiment in AI developer programming. ChatGPT generated every line of Flutter code.  The source code of Flapod can be found on GitHub.
Where do the photos and videos of Fapod come from?
Fapod displays the data from NASA's APOD website.
What is APOD?
APOD stands for Astronomy Picture of the Day, a daily image or photograph of our universe.
Where do the images come from?
The images are provided by NASA and other space agencies around the world.
